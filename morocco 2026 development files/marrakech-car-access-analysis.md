## üöó THE FUNDAMENTAL REALITY

**Cars CANNOT enter the Medina interior.**

The Medina consists of narrow pedestrian alleys, often 1-2 meters wide, designed for foot traffic, donkeys, and carts. No modern car can physically fit down most streets deep in the Medina.

**This affects:**
- ALL riads in the Medina center
- How guests arrive with luggage
- How car hire delivery works
- Which riads to choose

---

## üìç HOW GUESTS ACCESS MEDINA RIADS (Standard Practice)

### **Arrival Process:**

**Step 1: Park at Medina Perimeter**
- Park at one of the main gates (called "Bab")
- Common gates: Bab Doukkala, Bab Taghzout, Bab Agnaou, Bab El Ksour
- Guarded parking lots: ¬£5-7/day (60-80 dirhams)
- Street parking with attendants: ¬£3-5/day

**Step 2: Navigate to Riad**
- Walk through pedestrian streets with rolling luggage
- Distance varies: 100m to 500m+ depending on riad location
- Cobblestone streets (difficult with heavy bags)
- **CRITICAL:** Most riads send staff to MEET guests at parking and guide them

**Step 3: Staff Assistance**
- Call riad upon arrival at parking
- Riad staff comes to collect you
- They help with luggage and navigation
- This is STANDARD SERVICE, not special

## üöô CAR HIRE DELIVERY TO RIADS - HOW IT ACTUALLY WORKS

### **The Reality:**

Car rental companies **CANNOT deliver to your riad door** if it's deep in the Medina.

**What they DO instead:**

1. **Meet at Nearest Accessible Point**
   - Company delivers car to edge of Medina
   - Common meeting points: Parking near gates (Bab Doukkala, etc.)
   - They share live WhatsApp location
   - Meet you at agreed parking spot

2. **Coordination Process:**
   - Book rental specifying "Riad [Name], Medina Marrakech"
   - Company contacts you via WhatsApp
   - They determine nearest accessible point
   - Arrange meeting time (typically 8-9am for your departure day)
   - Driver arrives, you inspect car, sign paperwork
   - Drive away directly

3. **What This Means for You:**
   - On departure day (April 2), you carry luggage to agreed parking point (100-500m walk)
   - Meet rental company there
   - Inspect and collect car
   - Begin your journey

## üéØ RIAD SELECTION STRATEGY - CAR ACCESS IMPLICATIONS

### **TIER 1: Edge of Medina Riads (EASIEST)**

**Location:** Near main gates, car-accessible streets nearby

**Advantages:**
- Parking 50-150m walk
- Easier luggage transport
- Faster car delivery meeting
- Less deep-Medina navigation

**Examples of Edge Areas:**
- Near Bab Doukkala (North)
- Near Bab El Ksour (West)
- Riad Zitoun area (South - near Bahia Palace)

**Parking Options:**
- Dedicated guarded lots
- On-street with attendants
- Some riads have partner parking deals

### **TIER 2: Mid-Medina Riads (MODERATE)**

**Location:** 200-400m walk from gates

**Reality:**
- Longer luggage walk (10-15 minutes)
- More atmospheric/authentic
- Riad staff ESSENTIAL to guide you
- Car meeting still at perimeter

**Requires:**
- Good fitness for walking with bags
- Reliable riad staff to meet you
- Clear communication about parking location

### **TIER 3: Deep Medina Riads (CHALLENGING)**

**Location:** 400m+ walk through maze of alleys

**Considerations:**
- Beautiful, authentic settings
- Can be difficult to find first time
- Luggage transport is work
- No practical advantage for road trip start

**For car-based roadtrip:**
- **NOT RECOMMENDED** for Phil's trip
- Too much hassle on departure morning
- Staff assistance critical
