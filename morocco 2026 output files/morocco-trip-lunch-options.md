## GENERAL LUNCH TIPS

**Timing Strategy:**
- Eat lunch 12:00-1:00pm to avoid tour group rushes (1-2pm peak)
- Gas stations are genuinely good in Morocco - don't dismiss them
- Pack snacks always - dried fruit, nuts, biscuits from hotel breakfast

**Budget Guide:**
- Basic caf√©/gas station: ¬£5-6 per person
- Good local restaurant: ¬£7-10 per person
- Tourist restaurant with views: ¬£10-15 per person
- Resort/hotel: ¬£12-20 per person

**Food Safety:**
- Stick to cooked food (tajines, grilled meats)
- Avoid raw salads if unsure about water source
- Bottled water always
- Peel your own fruit

**Hygiene Check:**
- Look for busy restaurants (high turnover = fresh food)
- Check toilet cleanliness (good indicator of kitchen standards)
- Gas stations often cleaner than small roadside cafes
- Hotel packed lunches are safest option
